Bert Kreischer

About

Albert Kreischer Jr. was born November 3rd, 1972, in St. Petersburg, Florida.

Bert Kreischer, nicknamed "The Machine", is an American stand-up comedian, podcaster, reality television host and actor.
While attending FSU, Kreischer was featured in an article written by Rolling Stone naming him the "top partyer at the number one party school".
Kreischer is known for performing stand-up comedy while shirtless. He is also known for his storytelling; his most popular story is about how he allegedly earned the nickname "The Machine". The story revolves around how he inadvertently helped the Russian mafia rob a train while on a college trip to Russia.
Kreischer produces and hosts the podcasts Bertcast and Open Tabs and the cooking show Something's Burning. Kreischer also co-hosts the 2 Bears 1 Cave podcast with Tom Segura.
